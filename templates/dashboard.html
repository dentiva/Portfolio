Read before using these numbers
  • This tab applies one flat USD→INR rate to every transaction. Real filing requires the SBI TT-buying rate of the last day of the month preceding each transaction (Rule 115, IT Rules).
  • LTCG is computed at 12.5% without indexation (Finance (No.2) Act 2024, applies on transfers on/after 23-Jul-2024). Pre-23-Jul-2024 sales fell under 20% with indexation — not modelled here. The ₹1.25L exemption applies to domestic listed equity only, so it is not applied to your foreign holdings.
  • STCG is taxed at your applicable income-tax slab — shown as an amount only, not as estimated tax.
  • Holding-period split uses the 24-month rule for foreign listed shares. Edge cases around month-end can shift the LTCG/STCG boundary by ±1 day.
  • Surcharge & cess, DTAA foreign-tax credit on dividends — not applied. Sanity-check with a CA before filing.
